It's been quiet here, but I'm alive! Work is keeping me busy these days, and what's left over is split between building new stuff and writing about it. Writing gets very little.

The building side shows over on [xmit.dev](https://xmit.dev): [cc.me, and found.as grows a contact page](https://xmit.dev/posts/cc-me-found-as/) and [four more utilities on cc.me](https://xmit.dev/posts/cc-me-more/). Building in public what I need when I need it is super enjoyable.

Work also produced [blit.sh](https://blit.sh), terminal streaming to the browser in a single binary. And for my own productivity, I built [zz.surf](https://zz.surf), a tiling browser for iPad, and [moo.pcarrier.com](https://moo.pcarrier.com), my web-based coding agent. Combined, they allow a nice workflow on an iPad Pro.

I also redesigned [my home page](https://pcarrier.com), now a cleaner map of everything I make, and much lighter to load as I moved from `iframe`s to baked screenshots, tailored PragmataPro to only the glyphs I need. And I now carry an NFC keyfob that points to [found.as/p](https://found.as/p), so I'm drinking my own champagne.

More here when time allows.
